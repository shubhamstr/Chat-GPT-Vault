Privacy Policy

ChatGPT Vault (“the Extension”) helps users save, organize, search, and reuse prompts and responses from ChatGPT. We value your privacy and design our systems to ensure your data stays exclusively in your control.

Information We Collect

ChatGPT Vault does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share personal information with the developer or third parties.

The Extension only processes content that users choose to save from their ChatGPT conversations.

Data Storage

All saved data is stored locally on the user's device using the Chrome Storage API. The developer does not have access to this data.

This may include:

  • Saved prompts
  • Saved notes
  • User-created tags
  • User preferences

Website Access

ChatGPT Vault operates only on chatgpt.com and accesses conversation content solely to provide the following features:

  • Save prompts
  • Save responses
  • Organize saved content
  • Search saved content
  • Copy saved content
  • Export content to Markdown

The Extension does not access or collect data from other websites.

Data Sharing

ChatGPT Vault guarantees that we never engage in monetization or external sharing of your data. Specifically, we do not:

  • Sell user data
  • Share user data with third parties
  • Use advertising networks
  • Use tracking technologies
  • Use analytics services

Technical & Security Details

Remote Code

ChatGPT Vault does not download or execute remote code. All extension functionality is bundled within the extension package reviewed by the Chrome Web Store.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use third-party services for analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection.

Future optional integrations (such as Google Docs or Notion exports) will require user authorization and will be disclosed in this Privacy Policy before release.

Data Security

All saved information remains on the user's device unless the user explicitly exports or copies the content.

Children's Privacy

ChatGPT Vault is not directed toward children under 13 years of age and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published at this URL with a revised “Last Updated” date.
